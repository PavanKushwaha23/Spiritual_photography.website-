About The Project

Divine Visions is a spiritual photography project dedicated to capturing the timeless beauty and sacred energy of Hindu deities and temple architecture across India.

Through my lens, I seek to document not just the physical forms of our gods and goddesses, but the divine presence that radiates from these sacred spaces.

Each photograph is a meditation, taken during sacred ceremonies, golden hours, and moments of profound devotion to preserve the spiritual essence for generations to come.
